About

Who are we ?

Samloryx Consultancy is a consulting company based in Johannesburg, founded in 2022 with the purpose of creating a business value of IT through strategic planning and smart executions.

Rapid technology transformation and quick development are key to your success in this digital era and that’s the spirit behind Samloryx Consultancy.

Our aim is to help your business reach a higher level of success in this digital world. We’ve seen how solving organisations’ problems can help them move to the next level, and always be ready for the FUTURE. We are problem solvers at heart, and enjoy tackling even the most complex business challenges.

Our services are not technology or vendor specific, but rather focus on realising value by aligning processes, systems and people with business objectives. Because we are meticulous about understanding client requirements or pain points, we can design and facilitate change initiatives that bind together processes, supporting technologies, operational performance measures and the work system.

Portfolio

We assist our clients to implement technology and business roadmaps through: (i) Focused project and programme management. (ii) Provision of technical skills as required for system enablement. (iii) System development, implementation and integration. Furthermore, the technical capabilities are bolstered by end-to-end organisational change management, training development and skills transfer.
